Breakfast Bars

Prep Time

5 min

Cook Time

20 min

Total Time

25 min

Ingredients (8 servings)

  • 125g of nut butter
  • 180g rolled oats
  • 3 large, ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp chocolate pieces
  • 2 cups of granola
  • 1 tbsp of raisins
  • 1 tbsp of nut pieces
  • A pinch of salt

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit or 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. Mash bananas in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add rolled oats, nut butter and salt into the mixing bowl
  4. Mix to combine with large wooden spoon.
  5. Transfer mixture to a nonstick baking dish.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or check with a wooden toothpick, if it comes out clean it is ready.
  7. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 mins
  8. Once cool slice and enjoy!

Tip

Parchment paper can be used if baking dish is not a nonstick dish and it makes clean-up easier.
